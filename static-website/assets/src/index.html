About Plastic Waste

Plastic waste is a growing environmental concern. It pollutes our oceans, harms wildlife, and takes hundreds of years to decompose.

Facts About Plastic Waste

  • Over 8 million tons of plastic end up in the oceans each year.
  • Plastic waste can take up to 1,000 years to decompose.
  • 90% of the trash floating on the ocean's surface is plastic.

Solutions to Reduce Plastic Waste

There are many ways to reduce plastic waste, including recycling, using reusable bags, and supporting policies that reduce plastic production.
